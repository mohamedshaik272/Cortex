# Sample Queries for Cortex

## Diagnostics

- "My water heater is making a weird noise and my energy bill jumped 30%."
- "The AC is blowing warm air — what should I check first?"
- "I heard a loud pop from the electrical panel. What do I do?"
- "The sump pump ran continuously last night during the rain. Is that normal?"
- "My dishwasher is leaving dishes dirty even after a full cycle."

## Warranty

- "What's still under warranty in this house?"
- "Is my dishwasher covered if it breaks?"
- "My AC compressor failed — am I covered?"
- "When does the warranty expire on my furnace?"

## Financial

- "How much have I spent on plumbing in the last two years?"
- "What are my biggest home expenses this year?"
- "Show me a breakdown of my utility costs over the last 12 months."
- "How much did that HVAC repair cost me last summer?"

## Providers

- "Who did the AC repair last summer?"
- "Find me a plumber — who do I trust?"
- "I need an electrician. Who have I used before and how were they?"
- "Which contractor should I avoid and why?"

## Maintenance

- "What's overdue for maintenance?"
- "When should I replace my HVAC filters?"
- "Create a maintenance schedule for the next 6 months."
- "What's the oldest system in my house?"

## Proactive

- "Are there any local alerts I should know about?"
- "What's most likely to fail next in my house?"
- "If I had to budget $5,000 for home repairs this year, where should it go?"
- "Is my house ready for winter?"

## Summary

- "I'm selling the house — give me a full summary of all systems and their condition."
- "Give me a home health report."
- "What's the total I've invested in this house since I bought it?"
- "Generate a home inventory for my insurance company."
